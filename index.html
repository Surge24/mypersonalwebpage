So, who am I?

My name is Earl Wilbur A. Manglicmot and I was born on August 18th, 2007.I enjoy the creative arts, which include writing, music, and crafts. I enjoy traveling and discovering new places, cultures, and meeting new people. I am a grade 10 student at Mayapyap National High School, where I am able to participate in an arts program. The program includes Creative Writing (in both English and Filipino), Visual Arts, Music,Theater Arts, and Media arts. As a member of the instrumental music program, we get to play different kinds of music with my classmates and friends. It is always fun and enjoyable to play music with them.


Among my teachers, Ma'am Oma is one of my favorite teacher she always supports me in my academic and artistic endeavors. Also Ma’am Connie who taught me the html. This two are my favorite teacher’s.Overall, being a student at Mayapyap National High School has been an enriching experience for me, and I am looking forward to explore more in my artistic talents further and seeing where my passions will take me in the future.
